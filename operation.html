Aditi Mathur Kumar

Operation Payback by Aditi Mathur Kumar is a compelling military fiction novel that delves into the journey of a Veer Nari (war widow) who transforms her grief into a mission of national significance. Published by Penguin Random House India in August 2023, the book offers a narrative that intertwines personal loss with themes of resilience, patriotism, and empowerment. The story centers around Meera, a meticulous planner who envisions a serene life with her husband, Captain Ranvijay, a Para Special Forces commando. Their dreams are shattered when Ranvijay is killed in action during a mission in the Valley. Devastated yet determined, Meera chooses to honor his legacy by joining the Indian Army herself. Her journey is not just about personal healing but also about orchestrating a tech-driven, strategic operation aimed at delivering justice—not only for her loss but for the nation.

About the Author

Aditi Mathur Kumar is an accomplished author, advertising professional, and proud Indian Army wife. Her previous works, including Soldier & Spice and Love, Whatever That Means, explore the lives and experiences of army families. With Operation Payback, she continues to shed light on the sacrifices and strength of those connected to the armed forces.
