"The Great Indian Army: A Soldier's Life" by Khushi Ahir is a 32-page illustrated children's book that offers a glimpse into the life of an Indian Army soldier. Published by Orange Vtech Pvt Ltd on September 6, 2024, the book is designed to educate and inspire young readers about the bravery and dedication of soldiers. The narrative follows a young boy from a village in Rajasthan who, in 2004, learns about General Duty (GD) vacancies in the Indian Army. Motivated by this opportunity, he travels to Chittorgarh, successfully passes the recruitment process, and embarks on his journey as a soldier. The story highlights his experiences during training, the challenges he faces, and the sense of duty that drives him. Aimed at children in the early elementary to middle school range, the book serves as an educational tool to teach values such as courage, service, and sacrifice. It may also include fun facts about the Indian Army and its various divisions, helping children appreciate the diverse roles within the armed forces.
